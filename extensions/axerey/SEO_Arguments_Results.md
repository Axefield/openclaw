## Argument 2: What is White Hat SEO?

**Memory ID:** `98f3bbf7-53b5-427d-b6f3-404bdb15875d`  
**Confidence:** 0.4 | **Type:** Definitional

### Definition
White hat SEO refers to ethical SEO practices that comply with search engine guidelines. This definition emphasizes practices that prioritize long-term value for users and search engines, building sustainable organic visibility through transparent, guideline-compliant techniques. White hat SEO focuses on creating high-quality content, improving user experience, and earning natural links through merit rather than manipulation.

### Core Premises
1. White hat SEO practices comply with search engine guidelines
2. These practices prioritize long-term sustainability over short-term gains
3. White hat techniques focus on user value and natural link acquisition
