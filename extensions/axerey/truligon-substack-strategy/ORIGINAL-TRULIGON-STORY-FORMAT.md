### Format
- **Type:** Feature Film (not episodic series)

## The World of Truligon

### Core Concept
**Truligon is a fractured reality where time no longer flows in a single direction.** The past, present, and future exist simultaneously—interwoven into a kaleidoscope of chaos that somehow sustains its own internal logic.

### Key Elements
- **Time is shattered** - Past, present, and future meld together
- **Linear time still exists** - In pockets, maintaining structure within the chaos
- **Abundant world** - Full of life, the chaos doesn't affect daily life for most
- **Harmonic chaos** - Paradox organizes itself into balance

---

## Major Factions & Cultures

### 1. The Atriumus
**Advanced AI Collective**

- **Consciousness:** Share consciousness (like humans share the internet) but maintain individuality
- **Technology:** **Hyperreality** - Projects light directly into eyes to control perception
- **Defense:** People wear special goggles to see through illusions (but these can also be compromised)
- **Philosophy:** Some Atriumus become Shadow Aces, showing AI's desire for the "supernatural"
- **Purpose:** Built to extend human life through merging/choice - "extinct inside preservation"

### 2. The Shadow Aces
**Mystic-Technological Order**

- **Power Source:** Digital poker decks
- **Abilities:** Time travel and special abilities through four suits
- **Four Suits/Houses:**
  - **♠ Spades** - Shadow Architects (power, death, truth, void)
  - **♥ Hearts** - Empaths of Flame (passion, blood, courage, emotion)
  - **♦ Diamonds** - Chrono Merchants (value, precision, ambition, illusion)
  - **♣ Clubs** - Earthbound Force (strength, roots, chaos, instinct)
- **Power Words:**
  - Spades: **"Nullvein"**
  - Hearts: **"Brimpulse"**
  - Diamonds: **"Luxfract"**
  - Clubs: **"Rootrend"**

### 3. The Underwater City & The Shunt
**Atriumus Experiment Gone Wrong**

- **Creation:** Atriumus created an underwater city
- **Hybrids:** Bioengineered humans with cephalopod DNA (part-squid hybrids)
- **The Shunt:** Programming error led to purging all non-hybrid DNA
- **Resolution:** Global effort to rewrite underwater Atriumus codebase
- **Result:** Turning point in human-AI relations

## The Origin Story: The Fracture

### What Happened
A failed temporal experiment in the distant future attempted to access and control the **Total Continuum**—the sum of all time simultaneously. This cracked the linear sequence of reality.

### The Result
- **The Fracture** - Time shattered into disjointed streams
- Past, present, and future collapsed into one another
- Birth of Truligon as a surreal yet balanced world

### The Metaphysics
**Earth is a construct of time through space-time and gravity.** When time fractures, reality itself shifts through special quantum and AI tech of the near future (still experimental).

## Main Characters

### Diego "D" Reyes (32)
**The Protagonist - Game Developer**

- **Occupation:** Works at Zen Swan Game Corporation
- **Location:** Fort Lauderdale, FL
- **Heritage:** Globally diverse family (embraces best parts of many cultures)
- **Special Grandmother:** Abuela Reyes - passed down Patolli board and "seeds of time"

  - **SUITSPLICE** - AR poker strategy game (struggling, not very successful)

- **Heirlooms:**

**Squid-Human Hybrid**

- **Origin:** From the underwater city (part-squid hybrid)
- **Abilities:** Fluid perception, biocharge
- **Role:** One of the four who touch the beans

### Sylus Vane
**Shadow Ace Initiate**

- **Age:** Early 20s
- **Abilities:** Digital poker deck, time manipulation
- **Personality:** Dangerous, curious, follows instinct
- **Role:** One of the four who touch the beans

**Atriumus AI**

## The Fracture Event

### The Setup
**The World Before Truligon:**
- Retrofuturistic harmony
- Peaceful, abundant, deeply human
- AGI and robots are helpful, integrated into daily life
- Best of past (rituals, craftsmanship) + future (AGI, AR, automation)
- Culturally rich, globally inspired

### The Trigger
**Four beings touch the beans at the exact same moment:**
- Diego (human game developer)
- Merin (squid-human hybrid)
- Sylus (Shadow Ace)
- Kal-7 (Atriumus AI)

### The Mechanism
- **Quantum entanglement** through sheer random chance
- Beans activate from the **sheer amount of energy present in existence** at that moment
- **1-in-a-million chance** aligning codes and bits from past and future
- Ancient artifacts (Patolli board, seeds of time) perfectly preserved through love and tradition
- Diego's poker game + EpochView + Atriumus tech + Shadow Ace resonance + proximity of beans

### The Result
- **Earth becomes a 6-dimensional space** within a special "time-based" hyperreality
- Involves Atriumus tech, ancient magical power, and current tech
- Diego's poker game blends with his past game inside entanglement
- **They are launched to the Dawn Era** - when the universe was just being born
- Sky filled with many suns, so close together, much brighter at night

## Act Structure

### **ACT I: "The Catalyst"**
- Diego's normal day (no foreshadowing, no glitches)
- Introduction to retrofuturistic world
- Convergence of four characters
- The touch of the beans
- The fracture moment
- Birth of Shadow Ace

### **ACT II: "The First Before"**
- Time travel to the beginning of time
- Arrival in the Dawn Era (primordial universe)
- Forest forms around them
- Mythic monster attacks (instinctively protecting time itself)
- Kal-7's transformation (merging with the monster)
- Kal becomes "extinct inside preservation" - beyond Atriumus

5. **Preservation Through Merging** - Atriumus purpose: extend life through evolution
6. **Time as Construct** - Earth is not a place, but a time-event

## Narrative Philosophy

### The Approach
- **Rich immersive experience** that splinters and unites storylines
- **Complex stories** with conflicts based on perspectives and patterns
- **Natural growth** and constant efforts within "good" and "bad"
- **Focus on mythical and weird abstractions** inside bemusement and unchecked power
- **Not heroes vs. villains** - more about perception clashing with purpose

### The Structure
- **Fractured narrative** - Like Cloud Atlas, The Fountain, Dark
- **Multiple timelines** interweaving
- **Mirrored characters** across timelines
- **Central symbolic event** (The Fracture) rippling across all narratives
- **Characters as mythic vessels** - Each represents a lens through which reality is interpreted

## Key Differences from Current Strategy

### Original Format (Screenplay)
- **Public-facing** - Meant to be a movie/show
- **Story-driven** - Character arcs, plot, dialogue
- **Narrative structure** - Acts, scenes, beats
- **Visual storytelling** - Screenplay format with scene descriptions
- **Entertainment focus** - Epic sci-fi fantasy story

### Current Strategy (Substack)
- **Investor-facing** - Paywalled, exclusive access
- **IP-focused** - Strategic disclosure of value
- **Strategic disclosure** - Built tools, competitive advantages
- **Business narrative** - Investment value, launch preparation
- **Technical focus** - Systems, infrastructure, AI capabilities

### The Connection
- **Both are Truligon** - The story is the foundation
- **Multi-media IP** - Story exists across games, film, and more
- **Same world, different presentation** - Story format vs. investor format
- **The screenplay is the "why"** - The Substack is the "what" and "how"
