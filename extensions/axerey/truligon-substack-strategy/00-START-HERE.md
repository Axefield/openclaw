## 🎮 Why Substack for an Indie Game Studio?

### 1. **Own Your Audience**
Unlike social media, you control the relationship. No algorithm changes killing your reach.

### 2. **Deeper Engagement**
Email subscribers are 10x more engaged than social followers. They actually read your updates.

### 3. **Community Revenue**
Paid subscriptions turn passionate fans into sustainable support. Build games full-time.

### 4. **Build in Public**
Share dev diaries, art process, technical challenges. Transparency builds trust and excitement.

### 5. **Early Access & Feedback**
Paid subscribers get early builds, input on features, behind-the-scenes access. They become invested in your success.

## 💡 Key Principles

### 1. **Transparency > Polish**
Show the real process. Bugs, failed experiments, hard decisions. People connect with authenticity.

### 2. **Community > Audience**
Don't broadcast at people. Invite them in. Ask for feedback. Make them co-creators.

### 3. **Consistency > Perfection**
Better to publish regularly with "good enough" than wait for perfect. Momentum matters.

### 4. **Value First, Ask Second**
Give freely (dev diaries, art, insights), then invite support. Generosity builds goodwill.

### 5. **Long Game > Quick Wins**
Building a community takes months. Focus on genuine relationships, not vanity metrics.
