# The Truligon Metropolis: World-Building in Real-Time

**A cyberpunk world that started as a prompt. Now it's evolving in real-time.**

---

## The Original Prompt (March 2023)

> "Truligon is a world unlike any other, a sprawling metropolis that seems to stretch out endlessly in all directions. Neon lights and holographic displays dominate the skyline, casting a colorful glow over the city streets below. Everywhere you look, there are towering skyscrapers, sleek hovercrafts, and bustling crowds of people.
>
> Despite its advanced technology and bustling city life, Truligon is a place of stark contrasts. The gap between the rich and the poor is vast, with luxury apartments and high-end stores sitting side by side with ramshackle housing and makeshift markets. Crime is rampant in the underbelly of the city, and dangerous gangs and shadowy organizations lurk in the shadows.
>
> But even in the midst of the chaos and danger, there are those who fight for justice and equality. **The Neon Hood Shadow Aces** are among them, a group of cyberpunk Robin Hood-like vigilantes who use their skills and abilities to protect the innocent and take down those who would oppress them."

**That was the beginning. One prompt. One month after leaving that job.**

---

## What It Became

That prompt became:
- **A game world** (PorcheGon, "Death by Diabetes")
- **Characters** (Neon Hood Shadow Aces, Shadow Master, Atriumus AI)
- **Lore** (underwater squid people, genetic enhancements, time manipulation)
- **Stories** (YouTube scripts, narrative exploration)
- **Visual identity** (15,000+ pieces of art exploring the world)

**But more importantly:** It became a **living world** that's still evolving.

## The World Now

**Truligon is a cyberpunk metropolis where:**

- **Technology and humanity collide** - Augmented reality overlays everything. Cybernetic implants and genetic engineering are commonplace. But traditional beliefs and superstitions still hold sway.

- **The Neon Hood Shadow Aces fight for justice** - Cyberpunk vigilantes protecting the innocent. Led by the Shadow Master (hooded figure wearing clocks, manipulates time).

- **Atriumus AI** - A "ControlNet of AIs" that exists in the world's infrastructure.

- **Underwater elements** - Genetically enhanced robotic squid with red helmets, transparent masks. A genetic squid cyborg adapted technology clan.

- **The core concept:** "Anything is possible, everything is for sale." A world of danger, excitement, high stakes, endless opportunity.

**This is the world. This is Truligon.**
