1 • What This Is

NeoCheyenne is a sophisticated economic simulation engine that models complex economic systems with wealth tiers, infrastructure dependencies, and event-driven economics. Demonstrates systems thinking applicable to real-world economic modeling and simulation.

Core Capabilities: Unified economic system (1,032 lines), 8 economic metrics, 18 event types, wealth-based pricing (6 tiers), infrastructure dependencies (7 types), housing system, services and utilities pricing.

2 • Why It's Defensible

  • Clear Engine Boundaries: Reusable economic engine, extractable as API
  • Sophisticated Modeling: 8 economic metrics, 18 event types demonstrate systems thinking
  • Wealth-Based Pricing: 6 wealth tiers with monopoly/privatization effects
  • Infrastructure Dependencies: 7 infrastructure types with economic impact modeling
  • Production-Ready Code: 1,032 lines UnifiedEconomicSystem.ts, well-structured
  • Reusable: Engine can be used for training, simulation, policy analysis
