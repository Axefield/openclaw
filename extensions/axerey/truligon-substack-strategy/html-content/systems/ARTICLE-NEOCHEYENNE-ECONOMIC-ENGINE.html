Interconnected System

Wealth-Based Pricing: 6 Wealth Tiers

ServicesAndUtilitiesPricing.ts (462 lines) implements wealth-based pricing with 6 tiers:

Wealth Tiers

  • Tier 1 - Destitute: Lowest wealth, highest prices (monopoly effects)
  • Tier 2 - Poor: Low wealth, high prices
  • Tier 3 - Lower Middle: Moderate wealth, moderate prices
  • Tier 4 - Upper Middle: Good wealth, lower prices
  • Tier 5 - Wealthy: High wealth, competitive prices
  • Tier 6 - Elite: Highest wealth, best prices (privatization benefits)

Monopoly & Privatization Effects

  • Monopoly Effects: Lower tiers face monopoly pricing
  • Privatization Benefits: Higher tiers benefit from privatization
  • Wealth Inequality: Pricing reflects wealth inequality
  • Economic Impact: Pricing affects economic metrics
