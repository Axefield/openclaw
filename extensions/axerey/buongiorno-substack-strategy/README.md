## 💡 Key Principles

### 1. Evidence First, Always
- Lead with documentation, not claims
- Make everything verifiable
- Distinguish fact from opinion
- Provide source links

### 2. Non-Partisan Framing
- Constitutional rights protect ALL voters
- Technical security transcends politics
- Focus on process, not candidates
- Broaden appeal beyond party base

### 3. Transparency as Strategy
- Radical financial transparency
- Show where money goes
- Admit mistakes when made
- Build trust through openness

### 4. Consistency Builds Trust
- Publish on schedule
- Maintain quality standards
- Keep format recognizable
- Deliver what you promise

### 5. Sustainability Over Speed
- Don't burn out
- Build systems, not just content
- Batch production when possible
- Plan for long-term campaign

- Make unfounded claims
- Engage in actual conspiracy theories
- Lie to donors
- Violate platform ToS
- Attack individuals personally
- Retaliate against critics

- Cite sources
- Provide documentation
- Maintain transparency
- Distinguish fact from opinion
- Honor commitments to donors
- Stay focused on mission
- Respond to criticism with evidence
