Document #1: The Initial Complaint

Filed: February 15, 2022 | Palm Beach County Circuit Court

"Plaintiff alleges that the Supervisor of Elections failed to maintain ballot images in accordance with Florida Statute §101.68, resulting in gaps in the documented chain of custody for election materials..."
⚠️ What This Means:

In plain English: Every vote is scanned and stored digitally. Those scans must be preserved and tracked with detailed records showing who accessed them and when. Palm Beach County couldn't produce those records—which means they couldn't prove the system was secure.
