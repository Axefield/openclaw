## Executive Summary

This strategy positions Jeffrey Buongiorno as a technically qualified constitutional defender with documented evidence rather than another political candidate. The approach leverages his unique IT expertise and active legal filings to build a transparency-first campaign that transcends partisan divisions.

**Make Elections Constitutional Again** - Defending the American vote through technical expertise, legal action, and transparent evidence-based advocacy.
