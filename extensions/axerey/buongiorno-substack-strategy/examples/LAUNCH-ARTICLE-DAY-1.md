# Why I'm Running for Congress: Trust, Transparency, and Taking Back Power

## The Decision That Changed Everything

Three years ago, I discovered something that should concern every American who believes in the integrity of our elections. As a forensic investigator and IT expert with 30 years of experience, I was asked to examine election equipment and procedures in Palm Beach County. What I found wasn't just troubling—it was actionable.

I didn't just complain. I sued. And I won.

Now I'm running for Congress because the problems I uncovered in our local elections are symptoms of a much larger disease: **a system that protects itself at the expense of the people it's supposed to serve.**

## This Isn't About Left vs. Right. It's About Power vs. People.

For too long, we've been told to pick a team—Democrat or Republican—and fight for our side. But what if both sides benefit from keeping us divided? What if the real battle isn't conservative vs. progressive, but **establishment vs. accountability?**

I've seen it firsthand:
- **Election officials** who resist transparency because they fear scrutiny
- **Politicians** who promise reform but deliver excuses
- **Bureaucrats** who hide behind procedure when caught cutting corners
- **Media outlets** that ignore inconvenient truths that don't fit their narrative

This isn't conspiracy theory. This is documented fact. I have the court records to prove it.

## What Makes This Campaign Different

**I'm not a career politician.** I'm a forensic investigator, cybersecurity expert, and constitutional conservative who believes in solving problems, not winning sound bites.

**I don't just identify problems—I fix them.** When I found election security vulnerabilities, I sued. When officials stonewalled me, I persisted. When a judge ruled in my favor, I didn't stop—I ran for office to make systemic change.

**I'm running on four pillars:**

### 1. **Transparent Governance**
No more hiding behind executive privilege or bureaucratic opacity. Every vote, every decision, every taxpayer dollar—accountable to you.

### 2. **Electoral Integrity**
Elections aren't partisan issues. They're trust issues. We need verifiable, transparent, and secure systems that both sides can believe in.

### 3. **Constitutional Fidelity**
The Constitution isn't a suggestion. It's the rulebook. And Washington has been playing by house rules for too long.

### 4. **Anti-Establishment Reform**
I'm not here to make friends in the Swamp. I'm here to represent you—the people who are tired of being lied to, ignored, and exploited.
