# Fundraising Strategy - The 3-Tier Donor System

> Build a sustainable funding base across small, mid-level, and major donors

## Overview

Elections require money. This strategy builds three distinct donor tiers, each with appropriate cultivation, benefits, and asks. The goal is diversified funding that doesn't depend on any single source.

---

## 💵 TIER 1: BROAD BASE ($5-$25)

### Target Audience
- Social media followers who share content
- Casual Substack readers
- One-time contributors
- Small recurring donors
- First-time political givers

### Cultivation Strategy

**Through Content:**
- Evidence Drops with embedded CTAs
- Viral social media content
- Email campaigns with urgency
- Countdown timers for deadlines
- Match challenges when available

**Messaging:**
- "If 1,000 readers give $10..."
- "Your $25 funds [specific action]"
- "Small donors are our power"
- "Everyone can be part of this"

### Benefits

**$5 Donors:**
- Public thank you in weekly recap
- Email updates
- Gratitude + impact

**$10 Donors:**
- Everything above +
- "Supporter" badge in comments
- Monthly impact report

**$25 Donors:**
- Everything above +
- Quarterly strategy call access
- Early article access (30 min head start)

### Acquisition Tactics

1. **Emotional Storytelling**
   - Personal connection to mission
   - Underdog narrative
   - David vs. Goliath framing

2. **Urgency Creation**
   - Filing deadlines approaching
   - "72 hours to raise $X"
   - Court date countdown
   - Match challenges ($2-for-$1)

3. **Social Proof**
   - "$10K raised from 400 donors"
   - "Join 1,200 transparency supporters"
   - Donor testimonials
   - Progress thermometers

4. **Specific Impact**
   - "$25 = document retrieval fee"
   - "$10 = one hour of legal research"
   - "$50 = expert witness consultation"

### Campaign Ideas

**"Founding 1,000"**
First 1,000 $25+ donors get listed as founding supporters

**"Match Monday"**
Major donor matches all Monday gifts 2:1

**"Legal Fund Flash Drive"**
24-hour push for specific filing costs

**"Dime a Day"**
$10/month = change you'd spend on coffee

### Expected Performance
- **Conversion Rate:** 3-5% of email list
- **Average Gift:** $18
- **Recurring Rate:** 15% convert to monthly
- **Month 1 Goal:** 200 donors, $3,500
- **Month 3 Goal:** 600 donors, $12,000
- **Month 6 Goal:** 1,500 donors, $30,000/mo

## 💼 TIER 2: MID-LEVEL INSIDERS ($50-$250)

### Target Audience
- Engaged Substack readers
- People wanting deeper involvement
- Those with disposable income
- Professional class supporters
- Former political donors

### Cultivation Strategy

**Through Content:**
- Strategy Briefs showcase insider value
- Exclusive Q&A sessions
- Behind-scenes legal updates
- Early access to investigations
- Private community access

**Messaging:**
- "Be part of the strategy"
- "Insider access to the legal fight"
- "Your $100 funds real impact"
- "Join the advisory community"

### Benefits

**$50/Month Subscribers:**
- All Tier 1 benefits +
- Full access to Strategy Briefs
- Monthly strategy call (group)
- Private Slack/Discord channel
- Early access to all investigations
- Direct messaging access

**$100/Month Subscribers:**
- All $50 benefits +
- Quarterly 1-on-1 call with Jeffrey
- Advance copy of court filings (day before public)
- Input on investigation priorities
- Recognition in legal filings (optional)
- Exclusive monthly video briefings

**$250/Month Subscribers (Advisory Circle):**
- All $100 benefits +
- Monthly private dinner/call (small group)
- Direct input on legal strategy
- Early warning of major developments
- Named in acknowledgments
- Invitation to key hearings/events
- Strategic planning input

### Acquisition Tactics

1. **Demonstrate Value**
   - Show what insiders get first
   - Preview exclusive content in public posts
   - Highlight advisory input impact
   - Feature insider questions answered

2. **Limited Availability**
   - "Advisory Circle limited to 50 members"
   - "Next cohort opens [date]"
   - Waiting list for top tier
   - Exclusive community feel

3. **Upsell Funnel**
   - Tier 1 donors get upgrade offers
   - "30 days free at next level"
   - Special upgrade-only benefits
   - Anniversary upgrade invitations

4. **Professional Appeal**
   - "Networking with like-minded professionals"
   - "Executive briefings on election security"
   - "Be part of the solution at a strategic level"

### Campaign Ideas

**"Strategy Circle Launch"**
50 founding advisory circle members at discounted rate

**"Insider Week"**
Week-long trial of premium access for Tier 1 donors

**"Legal Action Partners"**
Mid-level donors fund specific legal motions, get named

**"Q4 Push"**
Upgrade challenge: match current monthly at higher tier

### Expected Performance
- **Conversion Rate:** 10% of Tier 1 → Tier 2
- **Average Monthly:** $120
- **Retention Rate:** 75% stay 6+ months
- **Month 3 Goal:** 25 mid-level donors, $3,000/mo
- **Month 6 Goal:** 75 mid-level donors, $9,000/mo
- **Month 12 Goal:** 150 mid-level donors, $18,000/mo

## 🎩 TIER 3: MAJOR DONORS ($500-$5,000+)

### Target Audience
- High-net-worth individuals
- Business owners aligned with mission
- Previous major political donors
- Those seeking significant impact
- Strategic thinkers wanting involvement

### Cultivation Strategy

**Through Relationships:**
- Personal outreach by Jeffrey
- One-on-one strategic discussions
- Private briefings on major moves
- Advisory role in decision-making
- Recognition appropriate to preference

**Messaging:**
- "Shape the strategy"
- "Your investment creates systemic change"
- "Be part of constitutional history"
- "Fund the legal battle that matters"

### Benefits

**$500/Month Advisors:**
- All lower tier benefits +
- Monthly 1-on-1 with Jeffrey (30 min)
- Named in major court filings
- Private briefing before public announcements
- Input on expert witness selection
- VIP event access
- Direct phone access for urgent matters

**$1,000/Month Strategic Partners:**
- All $500 benefits +
- Weekly text/email updates
- Quarterly in-person meetings (if local)
- Co-planning sessions on strategy
- Recognition in campaign materials
- Speaking role at events (if desired)
- Introduction to legal team

**$5,000+ Leadership Circle:**
- All benefits +
- Full strategic partnership
- Regular personal consultation
- Major decision input
- Public or private recognition (choice)
- Legacy naming opportunities
- Historical documentation inclusion

### Acquisition Tactics

1. **Personal Recruitment**
   - Jeffrey personally calls prospects
   - Individual coffee meetings
   - Private briefing presentations
   - One-on-one strategy discussions

2. **Network Leverage**
   - Ask major donors for introductions
   - Business community outreach
   - Professional association connections
   - Alumni network activation

3. **Mission Alignment**
   - Find those passionate about election integrity
   - Connect constitutional principles to personal values
   - Show systemic impact potential
   - Demonstrate serious approach

4. **Proof of Concept**
   - Show results from previous funding
   - Present legal victories enabled by donors
   - Demonstrate professional management
   - Transparent financial reporting

### Campaign Ideas

**"Founding Fathers Fund"**
10 major donors launch special legal initiative

**"Constitutional Defense Partners"**
Major donors fund specific constitutional challenges

**"Legacy Litigation"**
Major gifts fund landmark case with naming

**"Strategic Summit"**
Annual gathering of major donors for planning

### Expected Performance
- **Conversion Rate:** Personal outreach dependent
- **Average Gift:** $2,000/year or $200/month
- **Target Count:** 10-25 major donors
- **Month 6 Goal:** 5 major donors, $5,000/mo
- **Month 12 Goal:** 15 major donors, $18,000/mo
- **Year 2 Goal:** 25 major donors, $50,000/mo

## Integrated Tier Management

### Donor Journey

**Stage 1: Awareness** (Tier 1)
Social → Email → $10 gift → Monthly updates

**Stage 2: Engagement** (Tier 2)
Regular giving → Strategy access → Community → Upgrade

**Stage 3: Partnership** (Tier 3)
Major commitment → Strategic role → Legacy impact

### Movement Between Tiers

**Tier 1 → Tier 2:**
- Automatic upgrade offers after 3 months
- Special "Insider Week" trial periods
- Anniversary upgrade bonuses
- Impact reports showing insider value

**Tier 2 → Tier 3:**
- Personal outreach from Jeffrey
- Invitation to major donor events
- Strategic briefing opportunities
- Leadership circle recruitment

### Retention Strategies

**All Tiers:**
- Consistent impact reporting
- Personal thank you notes
- Recognition (when desired)
- Transparent financial accounting
- Regular wins to celebrate

**Mid & Major Tiers:**
- Quarterly impact reviews
- Annual strategy assessments
- Exclusive event invitations
- Personal relationship building
- Strategic input opportunities

- Weekly: Everything Tier 1 gets

**Tier 3:**
