# Publishing Cadence - The Consistency Engine

> Predictable rhythm builds trust, anticipation, and habit

## Weekly Schedule

### 📅 MONDAY: Evidence Drop (Lane 1)
**Time:** 7:00 AM EST  
**Content:** Court document analysis, new evidence, technical findings  
**Goal:** Start the week strong, maximize Monday morning engagement  
**Length:** 800-1200 words  
**Tone:** Professional, evidence-focused, authoritative

**Why Monday Morning:**
- Highest email open rates of the week
- Fresh start psychology
- Share-worthy for Monday workday discussions
- Sets agenda for week's conversation

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Document screenshot/scan included
- [ ] Plain-English summary provided
- [ ] Source citations clear
- [ ] "Why this matters" section
- [ ] Donation link top & bottom
- [ ] Social share buttons prominent
- [ ] Tweet thread prepared
- [ ] Cross-post to X within 15 minutes

---

### 📅 WEDNESDAY: Strategy Brief (Lane 2)
**Time:** 2:00 PM EST  
**Content:** Legal strategy, upcoming moves, tactical analysis  
**Goal:** Build insider community, drive paid conversions  
**Length:** 1500-2500 words  
**Tone:** Insider, strategic, transparent

**Why Wednesday Afternoon:**
- Mid-week engagement opportunity
- Subscribers looking for deeper content
- Time for Monday evidence to percolate
- Preview weekend media coverage

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Current situation report
- [ ] Strategic objectives clear
- [ ] Tactical timeline provided
- [ ] Obstacles addressed honestly
- [ ] Supporter role defined
- [ ] Email-gate or paywall set
- [ ] Upgrade CTA for free readers
- [ ] Comment engagement planned

---

### 📅 FRIDAY: Personal Story + Donation Drive (Lane 3)
**Time:** 4:00 PM EST  
**Content:** Emotional appeal, personal connection, urgent funding need  
**Goal:** Drive weekend donations, build emotional investment  
**Length:** 600-1000 words  
**Tone:** Personal, urgent, grateful, specific

**Why Friday Afternoon:**
- Weekend giving psychology (time to reflect)
- End-of-week decision window
- Paycheck timing (bi-weekly pay cycles)
- Lower competition for attention

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Personal story leads
- [ ] Evidence moment included
- [ ] Specific dollar goal stated
- [ ] Urgency explained clearly
- [ ] Impact statement compelling
- [ ] Multiple donation CTAs
- [ ] Progress thermometer if applicable
- [ ] Match challenge if available
- [ ] Thank existing donors
- [ ] Email subject line optimized

---

### 📅 SUNDAY: Weekly Recap (Lane 3)
**Time:** 10:00 AM EST  
**Content:** "This Week in the Fight" summary, wins, next week preview  
**Goal:** Retention, momentum building, weekly habit formation  
**Length:** 500-800 words  
**Tone:** Reflective, grateful, forward-looking

**Why Sunday Morning:**
- Weekend reflection time
- Lower inbox competition
- Church/community timing
- Planning mindset for week ahead

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Week's wins summarized
- [ ] Evidence released noted
- [ ] Community shout-outs
- [ ] Next week preview
- [ ] Donation link (soft ask)
- [ ] Engagement stats shared (optional)
- [ ] "Join us" invitation
- [ ] Social media recap linked

## Seasonal Adjustments

### Election Season (6 months before Election Day)
- Add Tuesday "Quick Update" posts
- Increase Friday CTA frequency
- Sunday becomes more urgent
- Wednesday adds polling/strategy analysis

### Off-Season (Off-cycle months)
- Focus on long-term investigations
- Deep-dive Strategy Briefs
- Community building emphasis
- Educational content increase

### Crisis Response
- Break schedule for urgent developments
- Same-day Evidence Drops when needed
- Real-time updates via email + social
- Return to schedule ASAP (consistency matters)

## Content Production Timeline

### Monday Posts (Evidence Drops)
- **Friday before:** Identify document/evidence
- **Saturday:** Draft analysis
- **Sunday evening:** Edit, prepare visuals
- **Monday 6:30 AM:** Final review, schedule
- **Monday 7:00 AM:** Publish
- **Monday 7:00-8:00 AM:** Social promotion, engagement

### Wednesday Posts (Strategy Briefs)
- **Monday evening:** Outline strategy update
- **Tuesday:** Draft brief
- **Wednesday morning:** Edit, fact-check
- **Wednesday 2:00 PM:** Publish
- **Wednesday 2:00-5:00 PM:** Engage comments

### Friday Posts (CTA)
- **Thursday:** Draft personal story + appeal
- **Friday morning:** Edit, optimize CTAs
- **Friday 4:00 PM:** Publish
- **Friday 4:00 PM - Sunday:** Monitor donations, thank donors

### Sunday Posts (Recap)
- **Sunday morning:** Compile week's activity
- **Sunday 9:00 AM:** Draft and edit
- **Sunday 10:00 AM:** Publish
- **Sunday 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Community engagement

## Consistency Rules

### ✅ DO:
- Publish at scheduled times (consistency builds anticipation)
- Maintain quality standards (don't sacrifice for speed)
- Keep format recognizable (readers know what to expect)
- Honor the schedule (builds trust)
- Batch produce when possible (efficiency)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Skip scheduled posts without explanation
- Change times/days randomly (confuses audience)
- Sacrifice evidence quality for urgency
- Over-promise publication dates
- Burn out (sustainability matters)

## Email Delivery Strategy

### Timing Optimization
- **Monday 7:00 AM:** Fresh inbox, high open rates
- **Wednesday 2:00 PM:** Afternoon break, engagement window
- **Friday 4:00 PM:** End-of-day, weekend consideration
- **Sunday 10:00 AM:** Morning reflection, low competition

### Subject Line Strategy

**Evidence Drops:** 
- "New Evidence: [Specific Finding]"
- "Court Filing Reveals [Shocking Detail]"
- "The Documents: [Date/Case]"

**Strategy Briefs:**
- "Strategy Brief: [Next Move]"
- "Inside Look: [Upcoming Action]"
- "What's Next: [Timeline]"

**CTA Posts:**
- "We Need Your Help"
- "72 Hours Left: [Specific Goal]"
- "This Week's Wins (And Next Week's Challenge)"

**Weekly Recaps:**
- "This Week in the Fight"
- "Weekly Wrap: [Date Range]"
- "What Happened This Week"

## Backup Plan

### If You Can't Publish On Schedule:
1. Send brief email explanation
2. Promise specific new timing
3. Deliver on promise
4. Return to schedule immediately
5. Build buffer content (bank 2-3 posts ahead)

### Emergency Protocol:
- Breaking news: Publish immediately
- Legal deadline: Priority over schedule
- Technical issues: Social media bridge
- Personal emergency: Transparent communication
