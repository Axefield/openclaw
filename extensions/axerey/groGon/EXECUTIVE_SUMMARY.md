## Company Overview

GroGon is a U.S.-based commercial drone manufacturer and service provider launching in Davie, Florida. We manufacture NDAA-compliant commercial drones to replace banned foreign-made products (DJI, Autel) and provide comprehensive drone services to local markets including real estate, construction, agriculture, and public safety.

## Market Opportunity

The December 23, 2025 FCC ban on foreign-made drones created an immediate market gap. DJI held 70-80% market share, leaving thousands of businesses without viable alternatives. The U.S. commercial drone market is projected to grow from $17.34 billion in 2025 to $65.25 billion by 2032, representing a 20.8% CAGR. Florida ranks 4th in drone profitability potential, with strong demand across multiple industries.

## Business Model

GroGon operates a dual revenue model:

1. **Manufacturing**: Four drone models ($3,000-$35,000) targeting different market segments
2. **Services**: Aerial photography, inspections, surveying, and training ($300-$3,000 per project)

This integrated approach creates competitive advantages: services validate products and fund R&D, while manufacturing enables cost-effective service delivery and builds brand reputation.

## Products

- **Commercial Inspection Drone** ($8K-$12K): Construction and infrastructure
- **Real Estate Photography Drone** ($3K-$5K): Real estate and marketing
- **Agricultural Monitoring Drone** ($15K-$25K): Precision agriculture
- **Public Safety Drone** ($20K-$35K): Law enforcement and emergency services

## Competitive Advantages

1. **NDAA Compliance**: Only U.S. manufacturers eligible for government contracts
2. **First-Mover Advantage**: Entering market during supply gap
3. **Local Manufacturing**: Faster response, better support, supply chain security
4. **Integrated Model**: Manufacturing + services creates defensible moat
5. **Market Knowledge**: Deep understanding of local South Florida market needs
